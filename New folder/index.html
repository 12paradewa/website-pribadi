Experiences

IT - ESEGA 2023

IT-Esega is an annual e-sport competition organized by HMTI and open to the public. This event has three branches of competition, namely PUBG Mobile, Mobile Legends, and Dota 2. In this activity, I had the opportunity to participate as a participant in the Mobile Legends competition. I was very enthusiastic about participating in this competition, which not only tests playing skills, but also team cohesiveness.

MUSANG HMTI2023

Musang HMTI, or Musyawarah Anggota Himpunan Mahasiswa Teknik Informatika, is an important event to evaluate the performance of the previous board and elect a new chairman for the upcoming period. In this activity, I was honored to be trusted as a member of the Publication and Documentation team, with the task of documenting important moments and conveying event information to all members and related parties.

BAKTI SOSIAL 2023

Baksos TI is a community service program conducted by Information Technology students to provide assistance to villages and schools in need. In this activity, I acted as a donor, providing donations to improve access to education and the welfare of underprivileged communities.
